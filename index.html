About Me

Here's a link to my CV (updated Jan. 2024). My research and teaching statements are available upon request.

I'm currently a CLE Moore instructor at MIT. I'm interested in probability and combinatorics, especially when algebra is involved.

Before moving to MIT, I obtained my PhD in mathematics, advised by Persi Diaconis, at Stanford University. Before that, I studied pure mathematics and statistics at the University of Waterloo.

I am co-organizing the MIT Probability Seminar and the MIT Integrable Probability Seminar.
